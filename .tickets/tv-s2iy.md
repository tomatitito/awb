# Add a persistent left ticket sidebar for Graph and Details views

Introduce a sticky left-side ticket list that remains visible in Graph and Details views, mirroring the codex-transcript-viewer navigation pattern and allowing quick ticket selection.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Graph and Details views show a persistent left sidebar listing tickets.
- Clicking a ticket in the sidebar updates the selected ticket and shows its details to the right.
- The sidebar supports the existing shared search and closed-ticket filtering behavior.
